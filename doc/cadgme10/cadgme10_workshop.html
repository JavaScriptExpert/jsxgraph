Worksheet Creator 2

Blended geometry for the class room

Michael Gerhäuser, Bianca Valentin,
Alfred Wassermann, Peter Wilfahrt
University of Bayreuth, Germany

Goals
  • Create Dynamic Worksheets based on DGS constructions together with questions, motivation, ..., accessible through the WWW
  • Enable use of resources from various DGS
  • Enable easy reuse/adaption of existing worksheets
Workshop Creator 2 (XWC2)
  • Online tool: http://jsxgraph.uni-bayreuth.de/xwc2
  • Free to use
  • Running on every device from PC to iPad
  • XWC2 creates one self-contained file with navigation
Supported DGS
  • GEONEXT
  • GeoGebra (mostly)
  • Cinderella (preliminary)
  • Intergeo
  • JessieScript
